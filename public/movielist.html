My Top 3 Movies of All Time

American Gangster

I love the aesthetic of this film, as I have always been enamoured by New York city. I love everything about it, the acting, costumes, soundtrack, character development. It is a perfect movie in my opinion.

Nightcrawler

Again, just the aesthetic alone made me fall in love with this film. The intensity, lighting, suspense, all tie together to make it one of my favourite movies
